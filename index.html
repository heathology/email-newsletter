Red Door Escape Room

In September, we took a group of the youth to the Red Door Escape Room in Chisholm Creek and we had a blast! We split up into two groups: younger vs. older. The older youth escaped our room and the younger youth almost escaped their room. Thanks to everyone who came out and hung out with us!

2022 Youth Pilgrimage Investor Dinner

The youth hosted an elegant and delicious dinner this past Sunday for those who donated and invested in the 2022 Youth Pilgrimage to Arizona. Between feasting and fellowship, stories and photos from the trip were shared, and our investors and stockholders got an up close recap of the June trip.
